Equipment We Have Available

This site updates regularly to showcase the new equipment that we currently have to offer such as Squats(Barbell), Preacher Curl(Barbell), Shoulder Press(Dumbbell) etc. Within the gym there are two of the same exercise equipment available for people to use. These range from shoulder exercises such as Lateral Raises or even Shoulder Press to Weighted Squats or even Leg Press for different muscles within your legs, to Preacher curls for biceps, Tricep Extensions for triceps and Cable Row for the back. For each muscle group there are different exercises that focus on a different part of the muscle, such as focusing lower and upper back on exercises or the long head and the short head on a bicep or the long-head for the tricep or side head for tricep. For the chest muscle there are three different parts that form the chest being upper, lower and middle chest and different exercises that will cater towards each of these different muscle groups. Whatever muscle you would like to workout Fitness First has it all. If you are unsure about the equipment we have please contact us using the email or phone number below and we will do our best to help.
